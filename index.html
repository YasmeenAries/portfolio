About

Hi, I’m Yasmeen Aries, a disabled, self-taught artist and lifelong creative. I first began with traditional mediums, building a strong foundation in technique and artistry. Initially, I was drawn to pencils, acrylics, oil paints, gauche, and watercolor, but as time went on I became enamored with digital art and design. My work currently centers on digital portraits and original stickers. I love to mix soft aesthetic and content with bits of edge, and my witchy vibe. I was attending school for graphic design until dropping out due to medical issues, but that didn’t stop me from creating! When not making art you can find me playing video games, checking out some new crystals, planning fall fits, and counting down the days until Halloween.
